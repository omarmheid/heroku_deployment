This website lets you choose between four different elements of happiness that we believe are an integral part of a balanced and happy life. If at the end of visiting our website you decide to move to the country of choice, remember that country was calling for you.

The 5 Elements of happiness in the human mind.

Carl Jung in an interview with Gordon Young was asked about the basic elements or factors for happiness in the human mind. Jung answered with these five elements:

  1. Good physical and mental health.
  2. Good personal and intimate relationships.
  3. The faculty for perceiving beauty and nature.
  4. Reasonable standards of living and satisfactory work.
  5. A philosophic or religious point of view capable of coping successfully with the vicissitudes of life.

The 4 Groups to Measure Happinness

We grouped those elements into four broad categories and collected 16 indicators from many countries around theworld. The following interactive sliders will allow you as a user to select the degree of importance each of these elements play a part in your life. Then we will surprise you with a country that suites your need for travel or to live in. There we are confident you find people who will be happy to see you.

Social Well-Being

Societal happiness .

Country indicators in this groupe, analyzes GDP per capita, the literacy rate, the suicide rate, and the birth rate per 1,000 or crude birth rate.

Go ahead use the range slider to indicate how important those factors are to you when choosing a country.

Financial Well-Being

Work Satisfaction and More.

Country indicators in this grouped analyzes of types of industries the population is engaged in. We analyzed the work force by country and collected information on the percent of the work force that is engaged in agriculture, service industry, manufacturing industry and self-employment.

Go ahead use the range slider to indicate which workforce type you would like to see in a country.

Mobility Well-Being

Perceiving beauty and nature around you.

Country indicators in this group are all about travelling and seeing beauty all around you. We selected indicators that show the percent growth of in country travel, the number of airport arrivals, the percent of coasline per square line and finally population density per sequare line.

Go ahead use the range slider to indicate your preference for what is important to you when considering a country to live or play in.

Physical Well-Being

Where and who we live with makes you happy.

In this group of indicators, we focused on the living conditions of the population within a country. We selected the types of household as a Percentage of all households by country. Those indicators are made up of the percent of the population that is female, the average size of the household, percent of individual who are living with a nuclear family and finally the percent of household that do not live with children.

Go ahead use the range slider to indicate your preference for what is important to you when considering a country population household living arrangement and percent of females.
